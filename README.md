Recent Advancements in Biotechnology: CRISPR and Gene Therapy

This repository contains my report on recent advancements in genetic engineering, completed as part of my CodeAlpha internship.

## About

The report looks at two of the biggest breakthroughs in modern biotechnology — CRISPR gene editing and gene therapy. It covers how these technologies work, what's changed recently (base editing, prime editing, CAR-T therapy, AI-assisted guide RNA design), where they're being used in medicine, agriculture, and industry, and the ethical questions they raise.

## Contents

- `crispr-gene-therapy-report.pdf` — full report with references

## Topics Covered

- How CRISPR-Cas9 works
- Base editing and prime editing
- Gene therapy delivery methods (viral and non-viral)
- Medical applications (sickle cell disease, hemophilia, cancer, etc.)
- Agricultural and industrial applications
- Ethical considerations and future outlook
